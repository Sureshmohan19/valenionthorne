My First Blog Post

January 29, 2025

Welcome to my first blog post! This is where I'll share my thoughts, experiences, and insights about various topics that interest me.

I've decided to keep things simple with this blog - no fancy frameworks, just clean HTML, CSS, and the beautiful Geist font. Sometimes the best solutions are the simplest ones.

Why Simple?

In a world of complex build processes and heavy JavaScript frameworks, there's something refreshing about going back to basics. This approach offers:

  • Lightning-fast loading times
  • Better accessibility
  • Easier maintenance
  • Lower hosting costs

Looking forward to sharing more thoughts here in the future!
